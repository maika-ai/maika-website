The science behind the Music

Music Micropauses at Work. Short music-supported breaks can reduce fatigue, improve focus, and support long-term resilience.

Music Micropauses at Work

See how structured micro-pauses support recovery, cognitive performance, and sustainable wellbeing in demanding work environments.

  • Recharge in seconds: even 30-second pauses lower fatigue and tension (Henning et al., 1997; Kim et al., 2017)
  • Boost focus & performance: micro-breaks improve attention by up to 15% (Ariga & Lleras, 2011)
  • Sustain well-being: regular pauses enhance vigor, motivation, and job satisfaction (Zacher et al., 2014)
  • Prevent burnout: short recovery moments help maintain long-term resilience (Trougakos et al., 2014)
